Hon. Abdoulie Njai

Champion of Community Development & Public Service

Biography

A dedicated public servant with over two decades of experience in community leadership and political reform.

“True leadership means empowering every voice in the community to contribute to our shared success.”
- Hon. Abdoulie Njai

Born and raised in Banjul, Hon. Njai has dedicated his life to improving living conditions, education access, and economic opportunities for Gambians.

Quick Facts

  • Born: January 15, 1980
  • Constituency: Banjul Central
  • Party: National Development Party
  • Education: MPA, Harvard University
  • Languages: English, Wolof, Mandinka

Career Milestones

2002: University Graduation

Earned Bachelor's in Political Science from University of The Gambia

2005: Harvard Graduation

Completed Master's in Public Administration at Harvard University

2006: Community Organizer

Started working as a community organizer focused on youth empowerment.

2014: Elected Council Member

Served as a council member in Banjul, advocating for local issues.

2017: National Assembly Election

Elected to the National Assembly, representing Banjul Central.

Key Achievements

Education Reform

Spearheaded initiative providing scholarships to over 500 students.

Healthcare Initiatives

Established a community health fund that improved local healthcare access.

Environmental Advocacy

Led campaigns for sustainable practices and community cleanups.

Community Engagement

Organized town hall meetings to foster community participation in governance.

Future Vision

Hon. Njai envisions a future where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. His goals include:

  • Expanding access to technology in schools.
  • Promoting policies that support small businesses and entrepreneurship.
  • Advocating for enhanced public transport systems in Banjul Central.
